My Story
Who I Am
I didn’t start coaching because fitness came easy — I started because I was living the busy work, busy dad life and slowly losing myself. Low energy, low confidence, hiding behind bigger clothes, and telling myself I’d “start soon.” The turning point came when I realised my kids were watching, and I was spending more energy avoiding change than fixing it. I rebuilt my body and mindset with simple, sustainable habits that work in real life — and now I help men do the same without extremes, burnout, or guesswork.

If you want to feel stronger, clearer, and more confident, book a free call today. We’ll talk through your goals, your schedule, and whether this simple, sustainable approach is the right fit for you long term, with guidance, accountability, and a plan built around real life.
