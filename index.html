The Programme
How it Works
A simple, proven process that guides you step by step, keeping training effective, manageable, and sustainable long term.
Step 1: Show up
Show up for one session per day. Don’t try to make up missed days by doubling workouts. If you miss a day, move on and get back on track.
Step 2: Move with intent
Every rep and every run has a purpose. Don’t rush lifts, and don’t turn easy runs into hard ones.
Step 3: Progress properly
Progress gradually instead of chasing ego. Increase weights with control — if form breaks down, the lift doesn’t count.
Step 4: Respect Zone 2
Zone 2 means easy. If you can’t hold a conversation while running, you’re pushing harder than you should.
Step 5: Warm up properly
Warming up prevents injuries and improves performance. Never skip it, no matter how short the session feels.
Step 6: Prioritise recovery
Sleep, hydration, and nutrition matter. Recovery will either support your progress or quietly hold it back.
Step 7: Track everything
Write things down. Track your runs, your lifts, and how you felt. Stay patient and keep showing up, the results will show.
