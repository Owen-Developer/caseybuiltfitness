WHAT IS THE 6 WEEK Back2Basic SHRED?

The Back2Basic SHRED is a structured, intensive 6-week training and lifestyle challenge built around three pillars:

  • Movement & Engine  focused sessions to build strength, conditioning and capacity using simple patterns like push, pull, squat, hinge and carry.
  • Discipline & Standards  daily actions that tighten your routines: steps, hydration, sleep, and doing what you said youd do.
  • Mindset & Identity  shifting from Ill try to This is just who I am now.

Each week has a clear theme and focus so you always know what youre working on, from resetting and building routine, to driving harder and finishing strong.

You wont get random workouts thrown at you. Youll follow a structured six-week journey that builds, week on week.

WHAT YOULL WALK AWAY WITH

By the end of the 6 weeks, you can expect to:

  • Have a simple framework you can repeat and build on after the 6 weeks end.
  • Feel fitter, stronger and more capable in day-to-day life.
  • Have clearer routines around training, sleep, steps and hydration.
  • Build discipline you can actually feel  not just talk about.
  • Look in the mirror and know you followed through on what you said youd do.
